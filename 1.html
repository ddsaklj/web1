tyranny of merit 공정하다는 착각

the notion that our fate reflects our merit runs deep in the moral intuitions of western culture. This way of thinking ( natural events happen for a reason) is the origin of merotocratic thinking. This is not far from the comtemporary view that wealth signifies talent and hard work and that poverty signifies indolence

focusing only on rising does little to cultivate the social bonds and civic attachements that democracy requires. it is often assumed that the only alternative to equality of opportunity is a oppresive equality of results. but there is another alternative: a broad equality of condition that enables those who do not achieve great wealth or prestigious positions to live lives of decency and dignity
